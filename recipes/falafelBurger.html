Falafel Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • Canned Chickpeas
  • Carrot
  • Red Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Flour
  • Spices
  • Neutral Oil

Steps

  1. Prep the ingredients
  2. Puree the ingredients to make a "dough" using a food processor
  3. Stir in the spices
  4. Form patties with the "dough" made from the ingredients
  5. In a frying pan, pour oil and add the patties
  6. Cook until golden brown, flipping as necessary, then serve
