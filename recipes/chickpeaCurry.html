Ingredients

  • Canned Chickpeas
  • Ginger
  • Yellow Onion
  • Garlic
  • Coconut milk
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Olive Oil
  • Spinach leaves

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil on a medium high heat.
  2. Add the garlic, ginger, spinach and saute for 2 minutes
  3. Stir in the spices
  4. Add in the tomatoes then add spices and chickpeas
  5. In a frying pan, pour oil and add the patties
  6. Slowly add in the coconut milk, until fully combined, then remove from heat
